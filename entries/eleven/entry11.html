11.
"...He really likes to eavesdrop on students....."

DATE

11/8/25, Saturday, around 8PM

SETTING

Parsons sewing room, some Chinese international students are working on their projects on a weekend evening.

PEOPLE

GIRL 1 is cutting fabric at a work table.
GIRL 2 has turquoise hair and is sitting across from GIRL 1, chatting with her while working.
GIRL 1
(Talking about their professor while cutting fabric)
(Name of her professor), he really likes to eavesdrop on students, especially when they speak Chinese.
GIRL 2
(laughing mischievously)
Ahh, then you have to say "Wow, this professor is so handsome!" Or "Next time we have to bring some snacks for this professor, he's too good."
GIRL 1 Aren't you just giving him a boost of self-assurance?
GIRL 2 Hahaha


~ SCENE END ~

日期
十一月八號，週六，大概晚上八點

地點
學校縫紉房，有幾個中國留學生週末在學校做作業

人物
女生一在縫紉房剪布料。
女生二染了孔雀綠頭髮，坐在女生一對面跟她聊天。
女生一
(一邊剪布料一邊談論她們的老師)
（老師的名字），他喜歡偷聽同學說話，說中文。
女生二
(調皮地笑著)
啊，那要說「哎呀，這個老師好帥呀！」或者「下次要帶點小吃給這個老師，他太好了。」
女生一
你這不就是安慰他嗎
女生二
哈哈哈


~ 場景結束 ~
