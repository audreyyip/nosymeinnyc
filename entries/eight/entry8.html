Entry 8: “Weed makes you broke and gay”

DATE: 01/11/25, around 9pm, Saturday of Hallo-weekend
SETTING: At Patacon Pisao, a small Latin American restaurant mostly for takeout in the Lower East Side
PEOPLE: Three guys in their twenties, wearing regular clothing the day after Halloween. They look like they’re about to head to some event. DUDE 1 has a moustache, and he speaks with a Turkish accent. DUDE 2 has dirty-blond hair and is wearing a wife-beater and a leather jacket. DUDE 3 has curly hair and is also wearing a leather jacket or a varsity jacket. They sit on small and tall circular tables facing each other.

DUDE 3: Dude last night would’ve been so fun if I didn’t black out

OTHER DUDES (in unison): yeahhhhh

(Talks more about drinking the night before)

DUDE 1: You guys ever danced sober at the club

DUDE 2: I don’t think I’ve even been to the club sober blud

DUDE 3: Me neither

DUDE 2: You definitely have bro,
didn’t you go sober that time we went to ____ club?

DUDE 3: Yeah, but I just leave

(Starts talking about smoking weed to pregame)

DUDE 2: Weed makes you broke and gay

DUDE 3: I don’t think weed makes you gay....
But it definitely makes you lazy

DUDE 1: Yeah, weed makes you lazy

(all eating in silence)


DUDE 1: What drugs do you guys think you’ll never try?

DUDE 3: Heroin, for sure

(talks more about drugs, before eating in silence again)
(Waiter brings food to their table, and DUDE 1 speaks to them in Spanish)

DUDE 2: Bro, you speak Spanish?

DUDE 1: Yeah, I was friends with a lot of people who spoke Spanish,
and I travelled a lot in Europe, and in South America

DUDE 2: Oh damn, that’s cool

(At some point, they all finish eating and leave, DUDE 3 says thank you in Spanish)
