Entry 10: “I hope you fall and break your nose while you’re at studio 51”

DATE

18/11/25, around 6 PM

SETTING

Four students hanging out in the school cafeteria and having dinner together, the cafeteria is buzzing with conversation and movement.

PEOPLE

DUDE 1 has bleached dreads wearing a white hoodie, speaking with a slight valley girl accent.
DUDE 2 is a white dude with a long Victorian looking face and long straggly black hair and is slightly balding, some bald patches are starting to appear. He is wearing a long black trenchcoat.
GIRL 1 has short brown hair and is wearing a beige fluffy cat hat, a purple Harley Davidson shirt and jeans.

DUDE 1: I hope you fall and break your nose while you’re at studio 51

DUDE 2: You could come

DUDE 1: Lowkey I think I’m thirdwheeling

DUDE 2: Just do what the video tells you to do

DUDE 1 (in an exasperated voice): Ohhh my godddd

DUDE 2 leaves with his black coat trailing behind him, his bald spot is visible at the back of his head.

The remaining three converse amongst themselves, talking about some TikTok trend.

DUDE 1: I do that in the shower but with my boyfriend

GIRL 1: EWWW TMI DUDE

DUDE 1 (in a sing song voice, using his hand to gesture a washing motion): Wash my belly ! Wash my belly !

GIRL 1 scrunches up her face in disgust, while DUDE 1 and the other friend laughs in the background.

DUDE 1: Hahahaha ohh….. I’m gonna cry….. I’m always TMI

SCENE END.
