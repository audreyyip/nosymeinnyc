English Version

DATE: 08/11/25, Saturday, around 8pm at night
SETTING: Parsons sewing room, some Chinese international students are working on their projects.
PEOPLE: GIRL 1 is cutting fabric, GIRL 2 who has turquoise hair is sitting across from GIRL 1 and chatting with her

GIRL 1: (Name of her professor), he really likes to eavesdrop on students, especially when they speak Chinese.
GIRL 2: Ahh, then you have to say "Wow, this professor is so handsome!" Or "Next time we have to bring some snacks for this professor, he's too good."
GIRL 1: Aren't you just giving him a boost of self-assurance?
